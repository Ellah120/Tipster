How Verification Works

Understanding the verification process and benefits

1. Complete 10 Trades/Tips

Complete 10 trades to demonstrate your skills. Each trade is recorded for transparency

2. Meet Required Win Rate

Your required win rate depends on your chosen odds tier. Higher odds tiers require higher win rates but offer better rewards

3. Maintain Your Status

Once verified, you must maintain your performance to keep your verified status. If your win rate drops below the required threshold, you'll return to verification status.

4. Earn Rewards

Verified tipsters earn a percentage of fees when other users copy their tips, based on their odds tier and performance.
